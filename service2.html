HOME CARE SERVICES

At Ivy Health Care, we are committed to providing exceptional home care that addresses specific medical needs with a high degree of professionalism and compassion. Our services are designed to offer comprehensive support tailored to the individual requirements of each client, right in the comfort and safety of their own home. Our specialist care encompasses a range of targeted treatments and procedures, ensuring that each client receives the best possible care suited to their unique health situation.


Specialist Care Offerings

Tracheostomy Care

Our tracheostomy care program is overseen by highly trained professionals who specialize in the management of both temporary and permanent tracheostomies. This service includes routine care such as the cleaning and changing of tracheostomy tubes, monitoring for any complications, and providing emergency care if needed. We focus on educating both clients and caregivers on how to handle the tracheostomy safely at home, ensuring a smooth and secure living environment.

Stoma Care

Adjusting to life with a stoma can be challenging, but our dedicated stoma care services aim to make this transition as comfortable as possible. Our expert nurses provide personalized care that includes proper stoma maintenance, prevention of skin irritations, and regular assessments to monitor the health of the stoma site. Additionally, we offer psychological support and practical advice to help clients maintain an active, fulfilling life post-surgery.
